About Us

Aparoksha, in its very literal meaning, denotes perceptible knowledge, something that is not invisible to the eyes. Welcome to the third edition of IIIT Allahabad's Technical Fest "Aparoksha'18". One of the most well known technical fests in North India, Aparoksha attracts participants from India's premier engineering institutions including various IITs, NITs and IIITs. Home to exciting events like CodeRed, Alkhwarizm, Perplexus and Grayhound, Aparoksha is marked on the calendars with eagerness by students across the country. So get ready for the 3 best days of the year, Aparoksha returns grander than ever!
