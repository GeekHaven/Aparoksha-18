About

Our event

StartUp Intern Fair is a flagship event held during Aparoksha. We at IIITA promote the culture of StartUps and wish to expand our horizons by making you a part of this grand family. We ourselves house some prominent startups and wish to inculcate the startup culture in the budding technocrats of the nation. SIF’s 2018 edition will be held from 16th-18th March during Aparoksha

>SIF provides a hassle-free and an aggregated hiring process alongside giving you a chance to unleash your ideas and innovation amidst the students.

For the

Startups

It’s a great opportunity for startups to hire best talents not just from IIIT Allahabad but also from other top institutes of national importance across North India like IIT BHU, IIT Kanpur, MNNIT Allahabad etc.

Startups will get the opportunity to hire skilled interns from various streams and colleges, technical and non-technical both alike.

Before the commencement of the interview sessions, a pitching session is held, in which you'll get a chance to pitch about your startup, internship profiles and requirements for them.

Your startup would be shown on the official website of the startup internship fair that is equipped with massive outreach amongst the students.

Startup culture

at IIITA - IIIC

IIITA is destined to produce employers rather employees and to widen the startup base in the institute, a dedicated Incubation Centre has been set up. The incubation centre provides an eco-system that helps to foster the culture of innovation and also provide all support to prospective incubates in setting up their business at various levels. monetary assistance apart from being able to hire from highly skilled college students are some of the best offerings of IIIC.
